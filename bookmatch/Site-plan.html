Home page


The goal

Why is necessary
According to the national institute of research of brazil, 44% of the population doesn't have the habit to read, and 30% never bought a book. The statics are of major importance before any explanation about this website, it is a well-known fact that the youth and the past generations consider the habit of reading overrated, it reading became a daily life obligation not a pleasured hobby.
The purpose
Bookmatch believes that every single book is a new adventure, and every single person has a book that is perfect for the moments of her life. People will be attracted to this website because it will look more familiar than new, it is a social media of books, where you can match similar movies to books, similar books to movies, and similar books to other books. The website can be used by parents who want to induce the habit of reading into their teenagers and preteens, Adults that are interested in reading but can't commit because is too out of their comfort zone, or don't have anyone to talk about books, Autistic people that are very selective with their interests and have a difficult time with change, so they struggle to find something familiar but yet new in smaller aspects or even Help teens that pass more time in stream platforms and social media, to engage in reading because of the website book selection. People will be drawn to this website because is versatile and has a unique purpose, style, and goals.
Expectations
The visitors also expect to find a safe and familiar environment where it looks more like a social media platform so they can exchange information with each other and be comfortable. Achieve their goals, without being overwhelming.
Interface
Home page webpage that looks like some streaming platforms, in the homepage will be displayed books and movies, most searched correlations between books and movies.

Bookmatch

The perfect matcher
Book matcher
Bookmatch is focused on readers and people who want to acquire the habit of reading but usually struggle to get out of their comfort zone. With that being said the expectation the users will have is, to find the user the perfect book.
Matcher interface
The main interface in the website is the book match searcher, the user inserts a book, movie, tv show, HQ, or interest, in the book match area to find the book recommendations.
Book information interface
Once it finds a similar book to your interests, the website shows a resume of the story, where to buy the book, reviews, and a recipe section where recipes of foods characters consumed in the book, are posted on each book review page.
What you need
Bookmatch is a matchmaker that connects the previous interests in a new book, which may be in most cases a new hobby and experience as well. To enjoy book matches to the fullest the only requirement each visitor must have is a previous preference for the content they wish to consume.
What lacks in the market
The website is unique, and the majority of the content that matches your interests nowadays is not as inclusive in all forms of interests, even in huge libraries companies as far as they can go is only to receive raw insights of the public interest, book match can be as specific as the user wishes for.
